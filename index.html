Captain J's Sweet Squad

Gathering for a taste of the town's best, the Sweet Squad brings a burst of energy to the heart of Captain J Takoyaki. From the first bite of our signature takoyaki to the last refreshing sip of our handcrafted lemonades, every moment spent here is a celebration of flavor and friendship.

  • Irresistible Flavors: Experience the perfect crunch and savory center of our authentic takoyaki, starting at just ₱50.
  • Refreshing Pairs: Wash down your favorite snacks with our creamy Halo-Halo or zesty lemonades, made fresh daily.
  • A Space for Everyone: Whether you're grabbing a quick bite or hanging out with the squad, our vibrant shop is the ultimate destination for foodies.

Are you ready to join the squad? Visit us today and discover why Captain J is the go-to spot for all-day breakfast, coffee, and the best takoyaki in town!

WHY US

Why Choose Captain - J

Authentic & Award-Winning Quality

Leo's TEAM

Choosing Captain J means treating yourself to some of the best-rated street food in Cebu. Our signature takoyaki is crafted to be perfectly crispy on the outside and creamy on the inside, staying true to traditional Japanese flavors. This commitment to quality earned us the Grab 5-Star Eats Merchant Award, a testament to the consistent excellence in food and service that our customers love.

Unbeatable "Sulit" Variety

Co-worker

We believe great food shouldn't break the bank. Captain J is the go-to spot for budget-friendly "barkada" deals, with takoyaki prices starting as low as ₱50. Whether you’re craving classic Octobits, Smoky Bacon, or Melted Cheese, our diverse menu and assorted bundles ensure there’s something for everyone to enjoy without skimping on the premium ingredients.

A Vibrant Community Hub

Customer's Feedback

More than just a quick bite, Captain J offers a welcoming, casual atmosphere perfect for any occasion. Whether you're visiting our Main Branch in Madridejos or our seaside spot at Kota Park, you'll find a space designed for making memories with friends and family. From refreshing handcrafted lemonades to our famous baked sushi, we provide a complete, satisfying dining experience that keeps our community coming back.
